DC40175 Decade Counter

A Arduino library for the DC40175 decimal counter

Pin Functions CD4017B

  • PIN 16 - V DC
  • PIN 15 - RESET
  • PIN 14 - CLOCK
  • PIN 13 - CLOCK INHIBIT (STOP COUNTING)
  • PIN 12 - CARRY OUT (INDICATE DECIMAL)
  • PIN 11 - 9
  • PIN 10 - 4
  • PIN 09 - 8
  • PIN 08 - GND
  • PIN 07 - 3
  • PIN 06 - 7
  • PIN 05 - 6
  • PIN 04 - 2
  • PIN 03 - 0
  • PIN 02 - 1
  • PIN 01 - 5
